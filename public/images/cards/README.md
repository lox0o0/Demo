# Card Images Directory

Place your card images here for the home page content sections.

## Image Files Needed

### ACTIVITIES Section (Top)
1. `predictions.jpg` - Player headshot with MVP graphics
2. `profile-upgrade.jpg` - Profile/user graphic
3. `prize-wheel.jpg` - Prize wheel graphic (colorful, game-show style)

### FANTASY & TIPPING Section (Middle)
1. `fantasy-get-started.jpg` - Trophy/brain quiz imagery
2. `tipping.jpg` - Fixture/calendar visual
3. `fantasy-team.jpg` - Team lineup/fantasy roster visual

### WATCH Section (Bottom)
1. `ben-hunt-highlights.jpg` - Action shot of player (Ben Hunt)
2. `broncos-storm-replay.jpg` - Stadium/match footage
3. `top-performers.jpg` - Stats graphic/player montage

## Image Specifications
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (recommended) or 4:3
- **Recommended Size:** 800x450px or larger
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Optimization:** Images will be optimized by Next.js Image component

## Fallback
If images are not found, the cards will display a placeholder icon.
